Prayer Force Wing

The Prayer Force is led by the Prayer Secretary and is responsible for:

  • Organizing Thursday prayer services across designated centers.
  • Supervising preparatory prayers, core prayers, and weekly dawn Gethsemane meetings.
  • Leading healing, deliverance, and intercessory teams.
  • Organizing semester prayer marathons and fasts.
Outreach Wing

Headable by the Outreach Secretary, key responsibilities include:

  • Planning and executing campus and community missions and crusades.
  • Following up with new converts, visitors, and inactive members.
  • Interpreting innovative methods for personal evangelism and discipleship.
Music Wing (God's Instrument)

Led by the Music President, the wing covers:

  • The Praise and Worship team & NUBS-KNUST Choir (God's Instrument).
  • Organizing worship retreats and semester music workshops.
  • Collaborating with Ministering Marshalls every 3 years for Inspiropera.
Ministering Marshalls (MM)

Led by the MM Head, functions include:

  • Coordinating drama, choreography, and spoken word performances.
  • Expressing the Word of God through creative arts during service and outreaches.
Organizing Wing

Subdepartments under the Organizing Secretary include:

  • Ushering: Seating, offertory, and welcoming guests.
  • Publicity: Media, posters, website, and public promotion.
  • Technical: Sound systems, instruments, and sermon recordings.
  • Transport: Vehicle maintenance and logistics.
  • Protocol: Guest minister welfare.
Bible Study Wing

Led by the Bible Studies Coordinator:

  • Prepares study manuals based on the semester's theme.
  • Organizes Baptismal classes and inter-wing Bible quiz competitions.
