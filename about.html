Identification of Faith

Our beliefs are anchored entirely on fundamental Christian doctrines and biblical truth as outlined in our constitution.

Note: We affirm the authority of the Holy Bible as the sole Word of God for guiding Christian faith and conduct.

We believe in the Holy Trinity (God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit) and in Jesus Christ as the only Saviour of humankind.

We believe in the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, encouraging members to freely exercise spiritual gifts and continuously bear the fruit of the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the absolute supremacy of God's Will over human desire, relying on the Bible as our ultimate spiritual compass.

Official Affiliations

NUBS-KNUST operates as an officially recognized student body affiliated with:

  • National Body: The National Union of Baptist Students (Ghana Baptist Convention).
  • Campus Body: The Students Chaplaincy Council (SCC-KNUST) Kumasi.
  • Mother Church: Grace Baptist Church, Kumasi.
